Admissions

Start your journey at Pamantasan ng Cabuyao. Explore our admission process and apply today!

Admission Requirements

For Freshmen
  • Completed Application Form
  • High School Diploma or Form 138
  • Certificate of Good Moral Character
  • Birth Certificate (PSA Copy)
For Transferees
  • Completed Application Form
  • Transcript of Records
  • Certificate of Transfer and Good Moral Character
For Foreign Students
  • Valid Passport & Student Visa
  • Authenticated Transcript of Records
  • English Proficiency Test

How to Apply

Step 1: Submit Application

Fill out the online application form and submit the required documents.

Step 2: Take Entrance Exam

Schedule and take the entrance examination at our campus.

Step 3: Interview & Enrollment

Attend an interview, confirm your slot, and proceed with enrollment.

Important Dates

Application Period
